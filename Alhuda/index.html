Al-Huda Islamic Center

Al Huda Mosque in Kent, Washington, serves as a hub for religious, educational, and social activities for the Muslim community in the vibrant Pacific Northwest. The mosque offers a diverse range of classes and lectures led by local scholars on a regular basis. These educational sessions provide a welcoming environment for individuals to deepen their understanding of Islamic teachings and practices. Topics covered in these gatherings cater to a variety of interests and levels of understanding. Whether you are exploring the fundamentals of Islam, delving into the Quran, or engaging in discussions about Islamic jurisprudence, there are classes designed to meet the needs of every attendee. The scholars and teachers leading these sessions are highly knowledgeable and experienced, ensuring a meaningful and enriching educational experience. Through these programs, Al Huda Mosque provides a valuable opportunity for local scholars and teachers to share their knowledge, foster community connections, and empower individuals to enhance their understanding and practice of their faith.
